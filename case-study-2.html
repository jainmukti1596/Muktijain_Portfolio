As part of the digital marketing team at Give, I contributed to building the paid media function, supporting Search, Discovery, Display, YouTube, and Meta campaigns focused on driving subscriptions and individual donations. I also worked on growing the website's Domain Authority, improving organic traffic, and lifting blog conversion by 43%. On the partnerships side, I coordinated co-marketing campaigns with brands including Flipkart, PhonePe, Swiggy, Paytm, Meesho, TikTok, BigBasket, and CRED.

Month-on-month, donation volume grew consistently across both subscriptions and one-time giving, with Meta fundraising campaigns built on creative testing and direct-response formats driving strong results across individual fundraiser campaigns.
