It started with wanting to help animals and somehow turned into a full freelance digital marketing gig. DAR is a small animal rescue shelter in Dharamshala, Himachal Pradesh, and when I came on board their digital presence existed but wasn't doing nearly enough. I rebuilt their strategy from scratch, expanded their Google Ads into the US and Australia to find dog lovers and donors beyond India, launched Meta fundraising campaigns that put real money into the shelter's hands, and worked on website improvements and blog content to build organic traffic and long-term audience growth.

The campaigns connected the shelter with donors across the US, Australia, and India, people who would never have discovered DAR without the expanded digital presence. For a small NGO running on passion and limited resources, that kind of reach makes a real difference. Every donation meant another animal fed, another rescue funded, another adoption story waiting to happen. Good marketing, good cause, good outcome.
