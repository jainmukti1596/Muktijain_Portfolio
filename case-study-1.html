I manage paid media for a leading European two-wheeler brand across 7 markets at Publicis Groupe, overseeing 45+ accounts and €1M+ in annual ad spend across search, social, and SA360. When the brand faced a significant mid-year budget reduction, I restructured the campaign architecture to protect performance — rolling out Performance Max across all accounts, consolidating fragmented campaigns to give Smart Bidding stronger conversion signals, and shifting towards high-intent, brand and model-specific targeting to improve efficiency.

The outcome exceeded expectations across all core markets. CPC decreased, CPL improved, and CTR increased, while conversion volume remained stable despite reduced investment. These gains were driven entirely by strategic restructuring and prioritisation, not increased spend.
